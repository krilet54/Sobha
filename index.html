Sobha Hoskote

At Hoskote, Bangalore

Sobha Hoskote is an upcoming mixed-use development by Sobha Group in East Bangalore. Spread across 48 acres, this project will feature 18 high-rise towers with premium apartments, along with dedicated retail and commercial spaces. The township is planned to create a complete living ecosystem where residents can enjoy modern homes, shopping, and workspaces in one destination. With more than 5,000 residential units, Sobha Hoskote aims to redefine urban living in Bangalore.
